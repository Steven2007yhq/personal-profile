Personal Story

My journey, growth and passion

I started my journey in coding and design with pure curiosity and enthusiasm. From writing my first simple HTML page to building complete interactive projects, every step has been full of exploration and growth.

What drives me is the desire to create things that matter — beautiful, functional, and user-friendly digital experiences. I love turning ideas into reality through code, and I keep learning new technologies to improve my skills every day.

Beyond development, I enjoy solving problems, sharing knowledge, and working on creative side projects. This portfolio is not just a showcase of my work, but a record of my persistence and passion for the tech world.

I will continue to move forward, explore more possibilities, and chase my dreams in the world of programming and design.
